# Product Vision

Watermelon is a desktop workspace designed to make extracting clips from
YouTube videos simple and visual.

Instead of downloading full videos and editing them later, users can
directly select the exact moment they want from the YouTube timeline and
download only that segment.

The application focuses on clarity, speed, and ease of use.

Users interact with a single workspace that combines video preview,
timeline editing, export settings, and download management.

The goal of Watermelon is to provide the fastest and simplest way to
turn any moment from a YouTube video into a downloadable clip.
